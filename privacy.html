Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Effective date: 1 January 2026  ·  Last updated: 1 January 2026  ·  App: SplitCircle (Android)

This Privacy Policy describes how SplitCircle ("we", "us", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using SplitCircle you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Account data: name, email address, profile photo (when you sign up with email/password or Google Sign-In).
  • Group & expense data: group names, member lists, expense descriptions, amounts, currencies, split details, comments, attachments you choose to add.
  • Invitations: email addresses of people you invite to a group.
  • Support requests: any information you send us by email.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device identifiers: Firebase Installation ID, FCM token (for push notifications), Android advertising ID (for ads).
  • Diagnostics: crash logs, performance metrics, app version, OS version, device model.
  • Usage data: screens visited, features used, anonymised event analytics.
  • Ad interactions: ad impressions, clicks, and frequency (handled by Google AdMob).

1.3 Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your contacts, SMS, call logs, microphone audio, or precise location.
  • We do not store payment card numbers — SplitCircle does not process real money transfers.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following sub-processors. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging, Analytics) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Sign-In — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Supabase (database & realtime sync) — supabase.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob (advertising) — policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Google Play Services (in-app updates, ad ID) — policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing

We share data only as follows:

  • With other group members: the expenses, balances, names, and avatars you add to a shared group are visible to every member of that group.
  • With sub-processors: Firebase, Supabase, AdMob — strictly to provide the service.
  • For legal reasons: if required by valid law-enforcement request.

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

5. Advertising & Ad ID

SplitCircle displays ads served by Google AdMob (banner, native, and interstitial formats). AdMob may use your Android Advertising ID and limited device data to deliver and measure ads. You can reset or limit ad tracking at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID or Delete advertising ID.
  • Within the app: ads cannot currently be disabled in the free tier.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained until you delete your account.
  • Groups you created: retained until you delete them. Other members keep visibility of historical expenses unless you remove the group.
  • Crash & analytics logs: retained up to 90 days.
  • Soft-deleted profiles: we mark deleted_at and purge associated personal data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR / CCPA / DPDP Act, India), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data (edit your profile in Settings).
  • Delete your account — use Settings → Account → Delete account inside the app, which permanently removes your Firebase Auth record and soft-deletes your Supabase profile.
  • Export your data — email support@splitcircle.app.
  • Withdraw consent for ads personalisation — via your device's ad settings.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

8. Children's Privacy

SplitCircle is rated PEGI 3 / Everyone but is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers operated by Google (Firebase) and Supabase in the European Union, United States, or other regions. By using the App you consent to such transfers, which are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

10. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards: TLS in transit, encryption at rest (Firebase & Supabase managed), restricted access keys, and ProGuard / R8 obfuscation in the release build. No method of transmission is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the change. Material changes will be announced inside the app.
