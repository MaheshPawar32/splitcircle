Delete your SplitCircle account

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

  1. Open SplitCircle and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and data are removed immediately.

Option 2 — Request from the web

Enter the email address on your SplitCircle account. We'll send a confirmation link to that address — tap it to permanently delete your account.

This permanently deletes your profile and personal data and cannot be undone. Expenses you added to shared groups remain visible to other members for their records.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, email, photo, preferences) and login.
  • Friend links, settlements you created, device push token.
  • Crash/analytics identifiers are dissociated and purged within 90 days.

Questions? Email support. See our Privacy Policy.
